So, what is this project?

As a student who had too much free time over the summer, I decided my time was best used learning new skills. Since I had just finished my first course in computer science in my most recent semester, I thought it'd be interesting to try my hand at web design and see what I could come up with. I found out I had a ton of resources at the tip of my fingertips through the internet, and began learning. This website is my first project in web design.You can find the main resources I used below!

My first step was to learn HTML and CSS from this great, free program. I was able to learn the basics of the syntax of both languages so that I could create functioning webpages and understand more advanced lessons.
