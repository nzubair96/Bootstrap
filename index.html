Why did I do this?

As a student who had too much free time over the summer, I was inspired to try and teach myself a skill.Since I had just finished my first course in computer science in my most recent semester, I thought it'd be interesting to try my hand at web design and see what I could come up with.I found out I had a ton of resources at the tip of my fingertips through the internet, and began learning. You can find the main resources I used below!

Code Academy

My first step was to learn HTML and CSS from this great, free program. I was able to learn the basics of the syntax of both languages so that I could create functioning webpages and understand more advanced lessons.

Learn more »

Twitter Bootstrap

As I became more familiar with the language, I started to explore Twitter's Bootstrap framework, which is a library of prewritten HTML and CSS that allows developers and designers create effective websites quickly.

Learn more »

Lots of Google

Finally, I Googled everything. Whatever questions I had about formatting, syntax, or creative ideas could be found with a quick Google search. There are endless forums available to help you on your path in web design.
